I'm Kashyap Sundararajan — a Google Ads Search Specialist focused on one thing: helping businesses acquire high-quality leads at the lowest possible cost.

I specialise in building and optimising high-intent Google Search campaigns for lead generation. My approach combines rigorous keyword strategy, smart bidding selection, and continuous data analysis to consistently drive down CPL while scaling volume.

In my professional experience, I identified critical negative keyword conflicts that were causing a significant CPL spike — resolved them, restored campaign efficiency, and improved conversion rates. That kind of structured, analytical thinking defines how I approach every account.

I'm fluent across the full lifecycle of a Google Ads account — from conversion tracking setup and attribution to bid strategy progression (Manual CPC → Maximize Conversions → Target CPA) and campaign scaling. If there's a lever to pull, I know where it is.
