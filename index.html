I'm Kashyap, and I've been running Google Search campaigns for over 3 years. I started PaidSearch Pro because I kept seeing the same problem — businesses spending serious money on Google Ads with very little to show for it.

Most of the time, the issue isn't the budget. It's the setup. Wrong keywords, messy campaign structure, bids that don't match the actual goal. I fix that. I go through accounts properly, find what's wasting money, and rebuild things so every rupee is working harder.

One thing I'm proud of — at a previous role, I spotted a negative keyword conflict that nobody had caught. It was quietly killing the CPL. Once I fixed it, the numbers came back. That's the kind of thing I look for in every account I touch.

I'm not an agency. You'll always be talking to me directly, and I'll always tell you what's actually going on with your campaigns — no fluff, no jargon.
